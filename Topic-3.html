Friendship 7

On February 20, 1962, 14:47:39 UTC a newly upgraded Atlas LV-3B 109-D lifted off from its newly built launch pad in Cape Canaveral. This flight was carrying astronaut John Glen aboard the spaceship friendship 7. This flight was to be the first orbital launch of an American into space.

The flight

After many delays after problems with the rockets fuel tanks and weather, on February the 20th the rocket still faced a half an hour issues with a faulty component in the guidance system, this was not the last of the issues as one bolt out of the 70 bolts that secured the capsules door had to be replaced, which took another 42 minutes. After this issue, a liquid oxygen value had to be replaced, which took a further 25 minutes. the rocket finally took off in foggy weather this proved hard to film as the rocket launched from the pad.
